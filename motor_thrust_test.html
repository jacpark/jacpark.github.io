Desert Sparrow

Motor Thrust Test

Team: Jacob Parks, Benjamin Branch, Johyl Green

Why are we doing this lab?

By determining the thrust of our motors we are able to see how much our quad is able to lift. Once we know the weight of the quad as well we can calculate at what speed the motors will begin to lift the quad into the air.

Materials:

Arduino uno

Brushless motor

LiPo battery

Our thrust test rig

Arduino IDE

Electronic Speed Control

Scale

Potentiomiter

Lab Procedure

Gather all materials

Write and upload some code so that the speed of the motor is based on the position of the potentiometer

Charge LiPo battery

Attach motor test rig to scale

Solder a connector onto an ESC so that it can be plugged into the battery

Attach a motor to the rig and plug it in

Turn up the pot to full power and check the scale

Repeat with the other 3 motors

Results

Motor 1: 1.1kg | 1.1kg | 1.2kg

Motor 2: 1.1kg | 1.1kg | 1.0kg

Motor 3: 1.1kg | 1.2kg | 1.kg

Motor 4: 1.0kg | 1.1kg | 1.2kg
